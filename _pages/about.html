Our Process

  1. Pick up

    Your waste is picked up by a certified driver and placed in to a purpose built container. The waste is then taken back to our SALSA certified depot in Harefield.

  2. Arrival at the Depot

    Once the waste arrives at the depot it is sorted and it is treated. It is then used in the production of renewable energy in the form of bio-fuel.

  3. Transformed to Bio-fuel

    Biodiesel is a low carbon alternative to diesel. Since it is made from waste oil it minimises carbon emissions, reduces harmful emissions and improves air quality for all.

  4. Back to the grid

    Once the waste has been transformed in to bio-diesel it is put back in to the grid. Hep Olis makes sure that the majority of waste oil is transformed and send back to the grid.
