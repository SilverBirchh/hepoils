We facilitate the collection of food waste by providing purpose built large or small bins. They are completely cylindrical inside which allows no areas for food to collect. The lids are secure reducing the likelihood of spillage and preventing release of odours.

Collection is fast and at times best for you. We provide a bin exchange service whereby the bins are taken away and clean sterilised containers left at the same time so you always have bins available. We will never let you down.

Food collected from you is taken to an anaerobic digestion Plant. Gases are extracted and these provide a valuable source of renewable energy in the form of electricity. The solid remains of the waste are used as a fertilizer to aid in the growth of crops. You can be assured therefore that almost 100% of the collected food waste is recycled.
